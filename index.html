ABOUT ME

I’m Natalia Garcia, a 28 year-old graphic designer originally from Bogota, Colombia but now living in Montreal, Canada. I believe in good design. Design that makes people happy. Design that is simple, modern, eye-catching and that pushes things forward. I believe that nobody can understand graphic design without the passion of those who are dedicated to this profession. Passion for art is in my blood. Color, composition and harmony in general are the basis of my work. My role is to create an appropriate combination of design and usability for an effective design. My work focuses on helping you communicate your message or idea. I love creating beautiful, simple and effective designs.
